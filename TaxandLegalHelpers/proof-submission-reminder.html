Proof Submission Reminder

Don’t miss your chance to save tax – submit your documents in time!

As a salaried employee, you need to submit investment and expense proofs to your employer to claim tax benefits under the Old Regime. Here's what to prepare:

🗓️ When to Submit

Most companies request proofs between December to January.

📄 Common Documents to Submit
  • 80C: LIC, PPF, ELSS, Tax Saver FDs
  • 80D: Health Insurance Receipts (self & family)
  • HRA: Rent Receipts + PAN of landlord (if rent > ₹1L/year)
  • Home Loan: Interest Certificate from bank/NBFC
  • Education Loan: Interest Paid Certificate
  • Donations (80G): Receipts with trust PAN
📣 Smart Tip

Set a calendar reminder for December 15 with this note: “Submit Tax Proofs to HR”.

🛈 This is a general reference. Always check your company’s official deadline and portal instructions.
