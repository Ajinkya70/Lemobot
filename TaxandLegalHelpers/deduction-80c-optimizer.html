What is Section 80C?

Section 80C of the Income Tax Act allows you to claim deductions up to ₹1.5 lakh on eligible investments and expenses. These reduce your taxable income under the Old Tax Regime.

This tool helps you understand how much of your 80C limit you've used and how much more you can invest smartly before the year ends.
