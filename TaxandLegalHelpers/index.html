Our Mission

We believe that tools meant to help you earn, calculate, or comply with basic financial obligations should be free — just like the internet was meant to be. Lemobot’s mission is to empower freelancers, employees, students, and small business owners across India with no-login, privacy-friendly, zero-cost tools to help them understand and manage tax, salary, deductions, invoices, and more. This isn’t a product — it’s a public service.


We believe tools for understanding your income should be just as free as tools for spending it.

Why We Made This Free

In India, most people either overpay taxes or avoid thinking about them altogether — not because they don’t care, but because the systems are intimidating. Filing income tax, understanding HRA, or even making a clean GST invoice shouldn’t feel like solving a puzzle.

At Lemobot, we’ve built these tools for ourselves, for friends, and now — for everyone. They're designed with one goal: clarity without clutter.

  • No logins
  • No ads
  • No upsells
  • No trial walls

Because your ability to understand your own money shouldn't be a paid service. This isn’t charity — it’s belief. We believe that if you serve enough people first, the right ones will support you later. And even if they don’t — we’ve still created something good.

— The Lemobot Team

About Lemobot

Lemobot is an Indian IT initiative focused on building honest, accessible tools for the everyday individual. Whether you're salaried, self-employed, a parent, a student, or just figuring it out — we create experiences that don’t require apps, accounts, or explanations. We believe open finance is good finance — and that India deserves financial tools that feel like utilities, not traps.

These tools are for everyone — not just accountants. No jargon. No downloads. Just help.

Which Tool Should I Use?

Not sure where to start? Here's a quick guide to help you choose the right calculator based on your needs:

  • Income Tax Estimator – Compare old vs new regime. Ideal for annual planning.
  • HRA Exemption Calculator – Calculate exempt HRA if you live in a rented home.
  • Take-Home Salary Calculator – Estimate your actual in-hand pay from CTC.
  • Advance Tax Estimator – Plan quarterly tax payments if you're a freelancer or have extra income.
  • TDS Finder – Unsure if TDS applies? This tells you based on income type.
  • 80C Deduction Optimizer – See how much of your ₹1.5L deduction limit is used and what’s left.
  • 80D Deduction Optimizer – Estimate tax savings from medical insurance premiums.
  • Home Loan Tax Benefit – Calculate savings on principal + interest of your home loan.
  • Proof Submission Reminder – Get checklist and deadlines for HR tax proof submission.
