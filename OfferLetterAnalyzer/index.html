Why We Made This Tool

Companies love to show a big CTC number — but that ₹20 LPA may be mostly variable, deferred bonuses, or ESOPs you’ll never see.

This tool helps you decode:

  • 💼 How much is truly fixed and paid monthly
  • 🧾 What’s dependent on performance or hidden clauses
  • 🚩 Whether an offer is stable — or risky

Built for job seekers tired of HR jargon and surprises.

— Team Lemobot
