About Radha Niwas

Welcome to Radha Niwas, a 2-bedroom vintage villa located in the heart of Lonavala city. With its charming interiors, peaceful garden, and nostalgic atmosphere, it offers a perfect blend of heritage and comfort — ideal for couples, families, or a quiet weekend with friends.

The villa includes a kitchen equipped with basic essentials such as utensils and cutlery, ideal for making tea or light snacks. While full-scale cooking facilities like a gas stove are not provided, guests will find plenty of excellent restaurants and food delivery options nearby to enjoy a variety of cuisines.
