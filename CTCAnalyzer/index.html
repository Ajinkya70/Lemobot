Why We Made This Tool

In India, the term “CTC” often hides more than it reveals. What looks like a ₹12 LPA offer might land you just ₹65,000/month — after deductions you didn’t even know existed.

This tool was built for:

  • 🎓 Freshers negotiating their first job offer
  • 💼 Mid-level employees switching roles
  • 👨‍💻 Freelancers comparing full-time offers

We don’t promise financial advice. But we do promise clarity. In a space full of jargon, hidden clauses, and “we’ll explain later,” this tool gives you real answers — now.

— Team Lemobot

Why This Tool Is Free

Other platforms bundle these features into premium products or paywalled HR software. We’ve made this available — for free — because we believe:

  • 💡 Transparency around salary = empowered professionals
  • 🛠️ Simple tools should be available to all, not just HRs
  • 🚫 No ads, no upsells, no signups — just clarity

This is part of our commitment to build clean, useful tools for real people — founders, freelancers, job seekers — not just corporates.

Use it. Share it. No strings attached.

Understand Your Salary

What is EPF, HRA, etc.?
  • EPF: 12% of your basic pay is deducted & matched by employer.
  • HRA: Part of your pay is tax-free if you pay rent.
How to Increase Take-Home?
  • Negotiate fixed pay higher, variable lower.
  • Use tax-saving deductions under 80C, 80D.
What Recruiters Don’t Say
  • CTC includes bonuses & hidden benefits.
  • Always ask for in-hand estimate before accepting.
