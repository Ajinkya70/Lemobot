📘 How Home Loan Tax Benefits Work

Under the Income Tax Act, home loan repayments offer deductions under two sections:

  • Section 24(b): Up to ₹2,00,000 deduction for interest paid
  • Section 80C: Up to ₹1,50,000 for principal repayment (shared with other 80C items)

💡 These benefits apply only under the Old Tax Regime. Make sure you’re choosing the right one.
