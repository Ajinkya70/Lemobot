Why Use This Tool?

Most startup founders in India struggle to price their idea or early-stage revenue. Investors ask for metrics you’ve never heard of. This tool is designed to give you a realistic, market-based benchmark valuation — instantly.

No signups. No spreadsheets. Just answers.

Why We Made This Tool

Most Indian founders are either guessing their valuation or depending on outdated pitch decks. But knowing what your business is worth shouldn’t require an MBA or investor access.

How We Calculate

We use a revenue-multiplier method, common for tech and D2C startups. It considers net revenue (after burn) over 12 months and adjusts based on growth.

Formula: (Revenue - Burn) × 12 × Multiplier
Higher growth can justify a higher multiplier.
